Delicious, varied, easy and great for sharing, Pizza is one of the best fast foods there is! Maybe some basil leaves, maybe some garlic, but that was it back in the days when it was first eaten! However, now this well-loved dish has many recipe variations! Perhaps you love yours Marinara style with seafood and garlic? That's available in Karachi for sure, and lots more other styles all available to order online too. from regular places like Broadway Pizza, to more typical Italian places.
